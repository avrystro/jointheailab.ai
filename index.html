What is Telegram?

Telegram is a free messaging app that doesn't track or sell your data. It's also the only platform where we can build AI tools right inside the conversation.

Setup takes 2 minutes.
  1. Download Telegram from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Open it and sign up with your phone number
  3. Close this popup and tap "Join the Telegram Channel"
  4. That's it. You'll get a welcome message with everything you need.
