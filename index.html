Why Telegram over WhatsApp?

Both WhatsApp and Telegram claim end-to-end encryption. But WhatsApp tracks who you message, when, how often, your entire contacts list, and merges it all with Facebook and Instagram for ad targeting. Your "private" conversations fuel Meta's advertising machine.

Telegram doesn't sell your data. Yet the real reason we use it is you can build AI tools directly inside the conversation. We're not just talking about AI - we're using it, live, in the channel. Bots that answer questions, automate workflows, and do things WhatsApp literally can't.

WhatsApp charges businesses per message and requires approval. Telegram's Bot API is free, open, and unlimited.
